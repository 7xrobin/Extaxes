Germany gives you €1,000/year tax-free on investment gains (€2,000 if married) — this is called the Sparerpauschbetrag. Accumulating ETFs also owe a small annual advance tax each January called the Vorabpauschale — your German broker handles this automatically by debiting your cash account. All figures are estimates — not tax advice.
